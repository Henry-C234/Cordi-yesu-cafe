Our Story

Crafting Memorable Dining Experiences

Founded in 2010, Éclat has been serving exceptional cuisine with a focus on seasonal ingredients and innovative techniques. Our passion for food and hospitality is evident in every dish we create.

Our chef, with over 20 years of international experience, brings a unique perspective to classic dishes, creating a menu that is both familiar and exciting.

We source our ingredients from local farmers and producers whenever possible, ensuring the freshest flavors while supporting our community.
