Booking FAQs

Common questions about booking your event

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for weddings and 2-3 months for other events to ensure availability.

What's included in the venue rental?

Basic venue rental includes tables, chairs, linens, basic lighting, sound system, and parking. Additional services can be added.

Can I schedule a venue tour?

Absolutely! We encourage tours to help you visualize your event. Contact us to schedule a personalized tour.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 90+ days before the event receive a full refund. Please review our complete policy for details.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. A 25% deposit secures your date, with the balance due 30 days before your event.

Can I bring my own vendors?

Yes, you can bring outside vendors. They must be licensed and insured. Some restrictions may apply.
