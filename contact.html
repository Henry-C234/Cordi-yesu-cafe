Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What's the best way to contact you for urgent requests?

For urgent requests, please call our main line at +1 (555) 123-4567. We have staff available during business hours and on-call coordinators for events in progress.

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For weekend inquiries, expect a response by the next business day.

Can I schedule a venue tour?

Absolutely! We encourage venue tours to help you visualize your event. Tours are available Monday-Saturday by appointment. Contact us to schedule your visit.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations via video call for clients who can't visit in person. We can provide a virtual tour and discuss all your event needs remotely.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Please include your event date, type of event, expected guest count, and any specific requirements. This helps us provide you with the most accurate information and pricing.
